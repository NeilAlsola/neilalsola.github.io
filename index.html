About Me

Neil Francis P. Alsola
BSIT Student | Aspiring UI and UX Designer

I am Neil Francis P. Alsola, a BSIT student at CTU Consolacion campus. I graduated from Mandaue City School for the Arts during my Senior High School days. Designing has always been my passion, and as a visual artist, I keep learning from others who have more experience. I try to get better every day, not just as a designer, but also as a person. My goal is to become a UI and UX designer because I want to create designs that bring change and improve people's experiences. I believe in learning, being humble, and growing, and I’m determined to keep improving, step by step, so I can create something meaningful in this industry.
